Mongoose helps us to create a connection between MongoDB and Node JS,
Its also a library of MongoDB
It's also called as Object Document Mapper(ODM)

Advantages of Mongoose versus native MongoDB are :

1. MongooseJS provides abstraction layer on the top of MongoDB that eliminates the need to use collections

2. Models in mongoose will help to perform bulk of work of establishing up default values for documnet properties and validating <i class="fa fa-database" aria-hidden="true"></i>
